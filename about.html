This website is a simple static site served by Caddy. It's self-hosted on a little Raspberry Pi that sits on my desk. Just HTML, CSS, and a tiny bit of JavaScript. The source code is viewable on my github at github.com/RyanNicoletti/ryannicoletti.net .

Indie Web

The indie web consists of websites created, owned, and maintained by individuals. It's a niche alternative idea to what most people know as the modern web. One of the main principles of the indie web community, is having ownership over your own domain, website, and data. I've always been a fan of the "web 1.0", before it was over taken by a few large social media and advertising companies and was more just a place to share information.

This website is my small contribution to the indie web. I use it to share things I write about and projects I'm working on, as well as to share other websites I find interesting.
